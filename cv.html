James Tyrrell

Senior Research Software Engineer and Manager | PhD Applied Mathematics

Employment history

Jun 2023 - Present Senior RSE and Manager at the University of Birmingham

Working with researchers through the university to develop high-quality software solutions capable of transforming their research ideas into results.

Major responsibilites:

  • Leading the techincal delivery of major projects on behalf of researchers and collaborators.
  • Overseeing projects led by other RSEs (e.g. through project management and code review).
  • Line-managing a team of five RSEs.
  • Facilitating the healthy provision and growth of the RSE Service.

Nov 2019 - Jun 2023 Software Engineer at Arup

Full-stack development of desktop applications for Windows and Linux systems as part of the Oasys LS-DYNA team, primarily in the following roles:

  • Lead developer for Oasys REPORTER (C++ / Qt / JavaScript)
  • Developer for Oasys D3PLOT (C / Fortran / JavaScript)

Major responsibilities: developing new enhancements, fixing bugs, training junior developers, designing QA tests, integrating third-party libraries into our software and keeping them up to date, handling support requests from clients, liaising with the Product Manager to discus the future vision for our software.

Feb 2019 - Nov 2019 Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Birmingham

Within the Chemical Engineering department.

Researching the application of Fast Multipole Boundary Element Methods (FMBEM) to granular flow, comparing results to those achievable using other similar methods (e.g. Discrete Element Methods).

Conferences and study groups

I was an active participant in RSECon2024 (supporting a team member in delivering the RSE Regional Groups SIG Meeting) and in RSE Midlands 2024 (giving a lightning talk on my experience of managing the RSE Service at the University of Birmingham).
